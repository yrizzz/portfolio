# Change Google OAuth App Name

## Steps:

1. Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com/
2. Select your project
3. Sidebar: **APIs & Services** → **OAuth consent screen**
4. Click **EDIT APP**
5. Change:
   - **App name**: `YrizzzDev` (instead of Portfolio)
   - **App logo** (optional): Upload your logo
6. Click **SAVE AND CONTINUE**
7. Click through the rest (no changes needed)
8. Click **BACK TO DASHBOARD**

Done! Next time users login, they'll see "YrizzzDev" instead of "Portfolio"

Note: Changes may take a few minutes to propagate.
